Scientific-Policy-Finance Interface Consultancy

Dr. Musaraj's consultancy is grounded in the recognition that the most consequential decisions in global health, food systems, education, and environment are made not by scientists alone — but by institutions that must act on scientific knowledge without the capacity to generate it internally. The knowledge gap between expert and decision-maker is where her work operates, and the arbitrage value she delivers is precisely the bridging of that gap under conditions of high stakes and institutional accountability.

All consultancy activities are formally classifiable under 72.10 — Research & Experimental Development on Natural Sciences and Engineering, covering R&D in natural sciences, engineering, biotechnology, environmental sciences, and interdisciplinary work.
